      Commander Identification

      Enter callsigns before the mission begins

      Mission Brief

      Commanders, welcome to Ramstein Flag. Your mission objectives are detailed below.

      Objective

      Capture the opposing Command aircraft to win the war.

      Aircrafts

      Fighter

      L-shape jump (like a chess knight). The only piece that can jump over others.

      Bomber

      Slides up to 3 squares orthogonally. Must rest one turn between moves.

      Recon

      Slides up to 3 squares diagonally.

      Tanker

      Moves up to 2 squares in any direction. Can carry one friendly piece (except Command) across the board.

      Command

      Moves 1 square in any direction. Protect it at all costs — losing it ends the game.

      Special Rules

      • Boarding: Move a Fighter, Bomber, or Recon onto your own Tanker to load it. The Tanker stays put; the boarded piece becomes cargo.
      • Deploy Phase: When your loaded Tanker moves, you must drop the passenger on an adjacent square — or skip with the "Skip" button.
      • Bomber Cooldown: A Bomber that just moved shows an "X" badge and must rest for one of its owner's turns.

      Scoring

      Each captured piece earns you points:

      • Fighter / Recon: 2 pts each
      • Bomber: 3 pts
      • Tanker: 4 pts
      • Command: 10 pts (and you win the round)

      Scores persist across games. The player with the lower score starts each new game.

      Controls

      • Click your aircraft to select — green dots show legal moves.
      • Click a highlighted square to move there.
      • Move onto an enemy aircraft to capture it.
